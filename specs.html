The Nintendo Switch Specs

Weight: The Nintendo Switch is approximately 0.66 lbs without joycons and 0.88 lbs with joycons.
Screen: Multi-touch capacitive touch screen, 6.2 - inch LCD Screen that is 1280 x 720
CPU/GPU: Nvidia Custom Tegra Processor
Storage: The Nintendo Switch Comes with a 32GB of internal storage, but you cane easily expand the storage with micro SD Cards that are up to 2TB.
Video Output: In Desktop Mode the Nintendo Switch Runs at 1080p, In Handheld mode the Nintendo Switch runs at 720p.
Battery Life: The Battery is a 4310mAh Battery that will last approximately 2.5-6.5 hours, but this estimate depends on the games that you are playing. In Zelda Breath of the Wild, the battery will last approximately 3 Hours.
